The Beatles

Here is a timeline of the Beatles:

  • 1960, May 5 - The Quarrymen change their name to the Silver Beetles
  • 1960, Aug 17 - Name changes to the Beatles
  • 1960, Oct 15 - The first time the final Beatles lineup plays together (recorded demo tape)
  • 1961, Jul 3 - The Beatles return to Liverpool (from Hamburg)
  • 1962, Jan 5 - Album My Bonnie released (first album)
  • 1962, Jan 24 - The Beatles sign a contract with Brian Epstein to make him their manager
  • 1962, May 9 - Epstein meets with George Martin (producer), signs the Beatles to record at Abbey Road for EMI
  • 1962, Aug 18 - Starr officially joins the Beatles (Best is fired)
  • 1962, Sep 4 - First recording session at Abbey Road. Record Love Me Do and How Do You Do It?
  • 1963, Nov 1 - The Beatles (No. 1) EP released
  • 1963, Nov 22 - With the Beatles album released
  • 1964, Jan 10 - Introducing ... The Beatles released
  • 1964, Jan 20 - Meet the Beatles! released
  • 1964, Feb 9 - First appearance on the Ed Sullivan Show
  • 1964, Jul 10 - A Hard Day's Night album released
  • 1965, Aug 6 - Help! album released
  • 1965, Dec 3 - Rubber Soul album released
  • 1966, Aug 5 - Revolver album released
  • 1967, Jun 1 - Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band album released
  • 1967, Nov 27 - Magical Mystery Tour album released
  • 1968, Feb-Mar - The Beatles go to India to study with the Maharishi
  • 1968, June 6 - Film Yellow Submarine is released
  • 1969, Jan 30 - Last public performance on the rooftop of Apple offices
  • 1969, Sep 20 - Lennon tells the band he is leaving
  • 1969, Sep 26 - Abbey Road album released
  • 1970, Feb 26 - Hey Jude album released
  • 1970, Apr 10 - McCartney leaves the Beatles
  • 1970, May 8 - Let It Be album released
  • 1970, Dec 31 - Beatles dissolved
